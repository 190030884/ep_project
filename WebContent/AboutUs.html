About Us

"The human body is the best picture of the human soul." We like to take that forward and assist college going students and young people on their holistic health ranging from diet, fitness, etiquette, grooming, internal health, mental well being, performance, strength and endurance etc. The application would also enable them to create their respective profile to check their criteria and track their progress in the chunks mentioned. The title could be "TeensTree" which interprets to be a platform that helps young ones to rejuvenate and grow up healthy and fit.
